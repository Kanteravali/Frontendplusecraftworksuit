Personal Data Pulsecrafts Collects from You

At Pulsecrafts, we believe that you can have great products and great privacy. This means that we strive to collect only the personal data that we need. The personal data Pulsecrafts collects depends on how you interact with our services.

When you create a Pulsecrafts account, purchase and/or activate a product or service, download a software update, connect to our services, contact us (including by social media), participate in an online survey, or otherwise interact with Pulsecrafts, we may collect a variety of information, including:

Account Information

Your Pulsecrafts account and related account details, including email address, devices registered, account status, and age.

Device Information

Data from which your device could be identified, such as device serial number, or about your device, such as browser type.

Contact Information

Data such as name, email address, physical address, phone number, or other contact information.

Payment Information

Data about your billing address and method of payment, such as bank details, credit, debit, or other payment card information.

Transaction Information

Data about purchases of Pulsecrafts products and services or transactions facilitated by Pulsecrafts, including purchases on our platforms.

Fraud Prevention Information

Data used to help identify and prevent fraud, including a device trust score.

Usage Data

Data about your activity on and use of our offerings, such as app launches within our services, including browsing history; search history; product interaction; crash data, performance and other diagnostic data; and other usage data.

Location Information

Precise location only to support services such as location-based features or where you agree for region-specific services, and coarse location.

We use your personal data only when we have a valid legal basis to do so. Depending on the circumstance, Pulsecrafts may rely on your consent or the fact that the processing is necessary to fulfill a contract with you, protect your vital interests or those of other persons, or to comply with law.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy
